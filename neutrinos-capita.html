IMPORTANT RISK DISCLOSURE

All investments involve risk, including the possible loss of principal. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

NEUTRINOS CAPITAL is a SEC-registered investment advisor. No guarantee of investment performance, profitability, or preservation of capital is made.

Market Risk

  • Stock market volatility and price fluctuations
  • Economic downturns and recessions
  • Interest rate changes affecting valuations
  • Inflation reducing real returns
  • Geopolitical events and market disruptions

Investment-Specific Risks

  • Portfolio Management: Market risk, concentration risk, manager risk
  • Private Equity: Illiquidity risk, valuation risk, leverage risk, exit risk
  • Hedge Funds: Strategy risk, leverage risk, counterparty risk, short selling risk
  • All Investments: Credit risk, currency risk, liquidity risk

Operational Risks

  • Technology failures and system outages
  • Cybersecurity breaches and data theft
  • Human error and internal fraud
  • Compliance failures and regulatory penalties
  • Business continuity disruptions

Systemic Risks

  • Financial system crises and contagion
  • Bank failures and credit freezes
  • Market circuit breakers and trading halts
  • Sovereign debt crises and defaults
  • Pandemic and natural disaster impacts

Performance Disclosure

  • Past Performance: Past performance is not indicative of future results. The 18.7% average annual return represents historical performance and is not guaranteed.
  • Future Performance: No representation or warranty is made that any investment will achieve any particular level of return.
  • Market Conditions: Investment returns are affected by market conditions, economic factors, and other risks beyond our control.
  • Individual Results: Individual client results may vary significantly from historical averages.

Investor Suitability

  • Accredited Investors Only: Our services are available only to accredited investors and qualified purchasers as defined by SEC regulations.
  • Risk Tolerance: Clients must have the financial ability to bear the risks of investing, including the potential loss of their entire investment.
  • Investment Horizon: Clients should have a long-term investment horizon (typically 5+ years) to weather market volatility.
  • Diversification: Clients should maintain a diversified portfolio and not allocate more than they can afford to lose to any single investment.

Fee and Compensation Disclosure

  • Management Fees: Management fees are charged regardless of investment performance and may reduce overall returns.
  • Performance Fees: Performance fees are charged only on profits above specified hurdle rates and may create conflicts of interest.
  • Other Costs: Additional costs may include custodial fees, transaction costs, and other expenses that reduce net returns.
  • Fee Impact: All fees and expenses should be carefully considered as they can significantly impact investment returns over time.

Conflicts of Interest

  • Revenue Sharing: We may receive revenue sharing from certain investment managers or custodians.
  • Soft Dollars: We may receive research and other services from broker-dealers in exchange for order flow.
  • Affiliated Investments: We may recommend investments in which we or our affiliates have financial interests.
  • Complete Disclosure: All material conflicts of interest are disclosed in our Form ADV Part 2A.
