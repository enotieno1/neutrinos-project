Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and getting started

What is the minimum investment amount?

Our minimum investment amounts vary by service type. Portfolio Management starts at $50,000, while private equity opportunities typically require $250,000 or more. We offer solutions for various investment levels.

How do I get started with NEUTRINOS CAPITAL?

The first step is to schedule a consultation with one of our investment advisors. During this initial meeting, we'll discuss your financial goals, risk tolerance, and investment timeline to determine the best approach for your needs.

What types of clients do you serve?

We serve a diverse range of clients including individual investors, families, corporations, and institutional investors. Our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client type.

How are your fees structured?

Our fee structure is transparent and competitive. Fees are typically based on a percentage of assets under management, with rates decreasing for larger account sizes. We'll provide a detailed fee schedule during your consultation.
