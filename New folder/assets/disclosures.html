Code of Ethics

Personal Securities Trading

  • All personnel must obtain pre-approval for personal trades
  • Access persons report quarterly holdings and transactions
  • Blackout periods apply during firm trading activities
  • Prohibition on short-term trading (less than 30 days)

Gift and Entertainment Policy

  • Gifts over $100 must be reported and approved
  • No gifts from clients or prospective clients
  • Entertainment expenses must be business-related and documented
  • Annual disclosure of all gifts and entertainment received

Business Continuity Plan

Emergency Preparedness

NEUTRINOS CAPITAL maintains comprehensive business continuity and disaster recovery plans including:

  • Alternate office locations and remote work capabilities
  • Redundant data systems and backup procedures
  • Emergency communication protocols for clients
  • Regular testing and updating of continuity plans
  • Cybersecurity incident response procedures

Custody and Safeguarding of Assets

Asset Protection

  • Qualified Custodians: Client assets held with SEC-registered custodians
  • SIPC Protection: Securities protected up to $500,000 per client
  • Insurance Coverage: Additional fidelity bond coverage
  • Regular Audits: Annual audits of custodial arrangements
  • Segregation: Client assets segregated from firm assets

Privacy and Data Security
