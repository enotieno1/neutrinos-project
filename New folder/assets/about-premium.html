Our Journey

Founded in 2002 by a team of seasoned investment professionals, NEUTRINOS CAPITAL emerged from a vision to democratize access to institutional-grade investment strategies. What began as a boutique firm managing $50 million has evolved into a premier investment powerhouse with over $15 billion in assets under management.

Our growth has been guided by an unwavering commitment to our clients' success. Through market cycles, economic uncertainties, and technological disruptions, we have remained steadfast in our dedication to preserving capital while seeking superior returns. Our track record speaks volumes: 16 consecutive years of positive performance, even during the most challenging market conditions.

Today, NEUTRINOS CAPITAL stands as a testament to the power of disciplined investing, innovative thinking, and client-centric approach. Our team of over 200 investment professionals spans the globe, bringing diverse perspectives and deep expertise to every investment decision we make.
